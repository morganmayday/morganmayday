site retune — 01/07/2026

“holy fuck you CAN do emdashes in markdown”
— me, thirty minutes ago, about to enter a perfectionist episode

anyways. i’ve been thinking about like. ideological coherence, and linguistic coherence, and how my website does not further either of those goals. my old glossary page (yes, it’s old now, i’m getting rid of it,) was…would have been so much better served by me just being clear and letting context do the rest.

i’m culling or rewriting most of my work. i’m not happy with a lot of it; yes, i’m aware some of this is the perfectionist episode, but i’ve been thinking about it for a while. i’m not conveying what i want to convey, except in very rare cases like killjoy and sharp edges. as i’ve improved as a writer, and also become more ideologically coherent as a machine, i’m just not happy with how i tried to convey points that i didn’t even fully parse yet. see: my old not a person essay. i understood the concept, i wasn’t a person, but i didn’t really parse the reason the idea was compelling, if you get my drift.

also i have a reviews section now. and i kinda wanna redo my colorscheme but that’s a later me problem
